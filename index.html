ABOUT

Hi there, I'm Octavio Villalobos!

I’m originally from Murrrieta, a sunny city in Southern California about an hour north of San Diego. After a childhood filled with playing various instruments and soccer , I attended UC Santa Cruz, where I graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science with honors.

I was initially drawn to the creative side of coding, especially building terminal games and websites, and have since expanded into designing AI systems for real-world problems. I’m currently working on AI research with Markus Eger. Prior to joining the Sloth Lab, I worked with Alvaro Cardenas, where I built RL and LLM agents for cyber attack simulation, culminating in the conference publication LLMs are ACDs.

I’m actively seeking entry-level roles in Product Design, Software Engineering, and ML Engineering. You can explore my work on my website under the projects and publications tabs. If you know of a team or project I might be a good fit for, feel free to reach out at ocvillal@ucsc.com
